How Items Work

Players can get items in a variety of ways, the most commmon is by opening the chests on the map. Different chests have different chances to give rarer items. There are a multitude of different items, each with unique effects that interact in different ways with each other.

Items can help players get stronger to progress through stages easier, but sometimes you will receive items that don't improve your build or even hinder your character. In those cases, players can scrap these items and use the scrap at printers to print new items.

There are 6 types of items, common, uncommon, legendary, boss, lunar, and equipment. Common, uncommon, and legendary all drop from world chests and can be found soup pots on the moon and in the bazzar. Soup pots let players trade in scrap or random items for the soup item. Equipment goes in the equipment slot and is an activatable item. Boss items have a chance to spawn when you kill their associated teleporter boss. Lunar items are found in lunar pods or purchased in the bazzar. Lunar items require lunar coins to purchase them; a rare drop that, unlike normal currency, doesn't reset after runs. All lunar items come with negatives, for example, shaped glass doubles all damage, but halves your total health.
