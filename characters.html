Commando

Health: 110 (+33 per level)
Health Regen: 1/s (+0.2 per level)
Damage: 12 (+2.4 per level)
Speed: 7 m/s

Commando is the most well rounded character, but also the most underwhelming. Commando's skills are basic but useful, and most items will stack well. Commando fails to deliver late game compared to other survivors because of his mediocre skills, but he combats this weakness with his reliability.

Huntress

Health: 90 (+27 per level)
Health Regen: 1/s (+0.2 per level)
Damage: 12 (+2.4 per level)
Speed: 7 m/s
Armor: 0

Huntress is the glass cannon, with some of the lowest health in the game, players must be cautious of taking any damage, but her skills make up for this. Huntress has the only primary skill that locks on to enemies for you, you can also fire while sprinting to dodge attacks and deal damage at the same time. Huntress is a volatile and aggressive survivor, but with the right build, this character is solid.

MUL-T

Health: 200 (+60 per level)
Health Regen: 1/s (+0.2 per level)
Damage: 11 (+2.2 per level)
Speed: 7 m/s
Armor: 12

MUL-T is a tanky, high-damage survior who has a plethora of ways to deal damage. The highest damage skill combinations are rebar/retool, and nailgun/power-mode. MUL-T is a slower survivor, so be careful as you don't have many escape options if things go wrong. MUL-T has one of the highest potential dps out of any other survivor, and his slow movement speed can be offset in late game with just a few common items, overall MUL-T is a fantastic survivor to pick up.

Engineer

Health: 130 (+39 per level)
Health Regen: 1/s (+0.2 per level)
Damage: 14 (+2.8 per level)
Speed: 7 m/s
Armor: 0

Engineer's turrets make him one of the strongest survivors in the game. Engineer's turrets use all of the engineers loot, so any items you pick up will also affect your turrets. This mechanic is especially good with the common item Bustiling Fungus, which spawns a healing zone after standing still. You can use this item to triple its effectiveness by placing both turrets within eachother's healing zone, and jsut standing still. Engineer is the embodiment of "work smarter, not harder", with the right items, you can stand still and obliterate any enemy in sight.

Rex

Health: 130 (+39 per level)
Health Regen: 1/s (+0.2 per level)
Damage: 12 (+2.4 per level)
Speed: 7 m/s
Armor: 20

The plant-robot Rex has a unique gimmick where some of their abilities cost health, but have no cooldown. When playing Rex you must find the balance between damage and health, as using Rex's most damaging abilities will also damage yourself. Many of Rex's abilities also give life-steal, making it easier to recover lost health. Rex is a sporadic, yet balance oriented character that can deal massive amounts of damage while healing up all of his lost health.

Health Regen: 1/s (+0.2 per level)
Damage: 15 (+3 per level)
Speed: 7 m/s
Armor: 20

Acrid is a hybrid survivor who blends melee and ranged abilities together. Acrid can use either poison or blight for his attacks. Poision is a strong dot like bleed, but it cannot proc items or kill, while blight is weaker dot that can proc items and land finishing blows. Acrid is a mixed bag, he can output a lot of damage with his poison and blight, but he struggles in other situations due to his limited ranged attacks.

Heretic

Health: 440 (+132 per level)
Health Regen: -6/s (-1.2 per level)
Damage: 18 (+3.6 per level)
Speed: 78m/s
Armor: 0

The Heretic is a very special character, because you can't choose to play as them when starting a game. In order to play as the Heretic, you must collect all 4 lunar items of heresy, which change your default abilities into the lunar abilities. Once all four heresy items are collected, your survivor will then turn into the Heretic. Unlike any other survivor, she has negative health regeneration, which causes her to slowly wilt away, and without any healing items, slowly die. Heretic is a late-game only survivor, because of the means required to play as her, and the items needed to offset her health drain, but with the righ built, she can become very powerful.
